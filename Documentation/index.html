Roles & Responsibilities

Our purpose
• We value constructive feedback. We will avoid being defensive and constructively give input.
• As team members, we will pitch in to help where necessary to help solve problems and work efficiently.

Team meetings
• We will hold a regular weekly meeting in the Clayton State Library to discuss progress, problems and provide solutions.
• Additional meetings can be scheduled to discuss critical issues or tabled items upon discussion and agreement with the team members.

Communication and Decision-Making
• We emphasize open and honest communication – there are no hidden agendas.
• We emphasize balanced participation of all team members.

Planning Project work & Progress
• We will be using various sharing tools such as Google Drive, and shared docs to collaborate.
• We will utilize all available on-campus resources during our physical meetings.
